THE CORE PROBLEMS
Problem 1: Textbooks Are Too Big, So Students Don't Read Them
Textbooks are 400-800 pages. Students open them, get overwhelmed by size and
density, and give up. Instead of searching their textbook, they take photos of
pages and ask generic AI. But generic AI doesn't know their specific curriculum,
so answers conflict with textbook content and professor emphasis.
Problem 2: Explanations Aren't Personalized
Some students learn through analogies. Others need step-by-step logic. Others
need visual diagrams. Generic AI explains everything the same way to every
student. Half the class understands, half doesn't — even though they're asking
the same question about the same topic.

Problem 3: Generic AI Hallucinates About Curriculum
Generic AI confidently explains things wrong. It doesn't know what YOUR textbook
says. It doesn't know what your professor emphasized. Students get answers that
conflict with their notes and textbook. They don't know who to trust anymore.
Problem 4: No One Tests If Learning Actually Happened
A student gets an explanation and moves on. They fail the exam. Why? Because
nobody tested them. Nobody identified that they understood the WHAT but not the
WHY. Every conversation resets. It's explanation theater, not actual learning.
THE SOLUTION
An AI tutor that:
- Retrieves explanations from YOUR textbook (not generic AI)
- Explains concepts in the way YOUR brain works (learns your learning style)
- Tests you to verify you actually understand (not just heard it)
- Remembers what confused you before (builds your persistent learning profile)
- Improves every interaction (gets smarter as you use it)
Result: Every student gets explanations tailored to how THEY learn, grounded in
THEIR curriculum, with proof they actually learned it.
